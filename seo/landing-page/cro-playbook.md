# CRO Playbook — the RMD offer & copy method

Service-agnostic copywriting + conversion method, extracted from the website-creation master (`offers/website-creation/master/RHILLANE_WEBSITE_v2_MASTER_FR_2026-05-25.html`, sections *Mathématique de valeur* / *Garanties* / *Bonus*). Apply this to **every** new offer master and landing page so the method outlives any single document.

> Framework basis: Alex Hormozi's *\$100M Offers* value equation, adapted to the Moroccan agency market (MAD HT, FR copy).

***

## 1. The value equation

> **Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

Every offer raises the numerator and shrinks the denominator:

| Lever           | Move                                 | RMD expression                                                   |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ↑ Dream outcome | Sell the result, not the deliverable | "Un site qui **travaille pour vous**" — not "un site de 8 pages" |
| ↑ Likelihood    | Proof + written guarantees           | Case studies, *garanties écrites*, performance pledge            |
| ↓ Time delay    | Contractual deadline                 | Délai contractuel par forfait (3–16 sem.)                        |
| ↓ Effort        | Done-for-you, fixed price            | Prix fixe, build-up transparent, care plan post-launch           |

***

## 2. Offer master — required sections

Each service master is a comprehensive playbook (sales + delivery + marketing share one source of truth). Reproduce this spine per service:

1. **Vue d'ensemble** — forfaits, guarantees, bonuses, price range at a glance
2. **Fondamentaux** — positioning, avatars, pain points
3. **Catalogue de services** — the deliverable tiers
4. **Tarification** — forfait grid + entry products
5. **Modèle build-up** — line-by-line cost decomposition (kills price objections by showing the math)
6. **Garanties** — written commitments (see §4)
7. **Mathématique de valeur** — value multipliers vs market (see §3)
8. **Bonus** — stacked value-adds with attributed MAD value
9. **Filtrage & qualification** — lead funnel
10. **Appel de découverte** → **Pitch** → **Scripts d'objection** → **Closes** — the sales motion
11. **Copy LP / VSL / email / LinkedIn** — channel adaptations
12. **Opérations / arbres de décision** — delivery

The **landing page** is a CRO condensation of this master, not a new document.

***

## 3. Value multipliers (anchor every price)

State each tier's value as a multiple of comparable market cost. Reference (website-creation):

| Forfait      | RMD price     | Multiplier vs comparable   |
| ------------ | ------------- | -------------------------- |
| Présence     | 36 000 MAD HT | **1.9×** (\~70K FR agency) |
| Croissance ★ | 73 000        | **2.7×** (\~195K)          |
| Boutique     | 81 200        | **3.7×** (\~300K)          |
| Signature    | 120 000+      | **5×** (\~600K)            |

Always present price **after** value, never before.

***

## 4. Guarantees (perceived-likelihood lever)

Write commitments, not adjectives. Website-creation's 3+1:

* **G1 — Délai contractuel** : on-time delivery, written into the contract.
* **G2 — Validation design** : you sign off on the design before build.
* **G3 — Support 30 jours** post-launch.
* **+ Option performance** : PageSpeed mobile ≥ 90 (2 000 MAD HT add-on).

Each new service needs its own 3+1 expressed as measurable promises.

***

## 5. The landing-page funnel (maps to the 12 blocks)

1. **Pain reframe** (hero H1) — name the cost of the status quo.
2. **Free value stack** — lead magnet quantified in MAD (e.g. audit offert \~8 000 MAD).
3. **Trust** — partner marquee / proof.
4. **Problem stack** — 4 grievances the prospect feels.
5. **Mechanism** — the method (why it works), as a timeline.
6. **Engagements** — the guarantees, visually.
7. **Proof** — selected work with real before/after stats.
8. **Offer** — transparent pricing with build-up.
9. **Risk reversal** — guarantees grid.
10. **Objection handling** — FAQ (price · deadline · overruns · stack · post-launch · design).
11. **Close** — recap + single CTA.
12. **Footer** — navigation, external links `target="_blank"`.

See module layouts in [`../../../design-system/components.md`](../../../design-system/components.md).

***

## 6. Copy rules (non-negotiable)

* **FR** for all client-facing copy; **MAD HT** currency exclusively.
* **No red for negatives** — slate gray (`slight`). `redder` is premium emphasis only.
* Sell outcomes; quantify every claim (MAD value, %, days, multiplier).
* One gradient keyword per heading. No hype words without a number behind them.
* Don't re-introduce removed items: hosting on Présence (client-borne), CRM (not an RMD service).
